What Are HEMIS Credits?

You might have heard about HEMIS credits, but why are they important?

HEMIS Credits:

Each module at Stellenbosch Univerity has a Credit value.
The more modules a student takes and passes, the more Credits they accuire. If a student fails a module, they do not get any Credits from that module.
Go to the "Calculating HEMIS Credits" to find out more.
